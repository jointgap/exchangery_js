The RFE Program

The Exchangery is a platform as a service provider, which means that for us to succeed our customers need to succeed. To that end we have identified several market opporunities that we believe are particularly compelling and would make great exchanges. We are so excited about these areas in particular that we are willing to offer consulting and waive a portion of our fees to whoever builds an Exchangery exchange around one of these ideas. If you think that you or your company are up to the task, please contact us.

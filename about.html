About Us

Our Story
As veterans of the Chicago Climate Exchange, we saw first hand the process of taking a set of innovative commodities to market. We developed expertise in developing new contracts, helping non-traders understand the benefits of markets, and integrating with financial, regulatory and banking institutions. We brought that experience to the Exchangery, where we offer a packaged exchange solution.
Excelerate
Exchangery was selected in to the 2011 class of Excelerate, a Chicago based startup incubator. We received seed capital along with 3 months of mentoring, learning sessions, presentations from industry luminaries and investor introductions.
The Team

Chris Duesing - Cofounder / CEO

Chris Duesing has over 10 years of experience in software and technical services with a specialization in high throughput, distributed applications in the financial and retail industries. Most recently as the Director of Application Development for the Chicago Climate Exchange, Chris has spent the last 6 years focusing on entrepreneurship and developing new businesses. Chris is active in the Chicago tech community where he is an organizer of the Chicago Erlang Users group and as a mentor for ScaleWell.

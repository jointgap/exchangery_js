The Exchangery offers a unique approach to starting a commodities exchange.

We provide the technology, infrastructure, regulatory and clearing relationships as well as brandable trading screens that you can use to create a distinct, personalized online presence for your exchange.

It’s Your Exchange

We get that, so we designed our systems to be customizable and brandable. You can use our hosted trading screen with your own logo and design, or you can even use our APIs and libraries to add exchange functionality directly to your existing web site or application.

Our Solid Infrastructure

Our technology consists of scalable, fault tolerant components built with distribution in mind. We are not limited by expensive specialty hardware, so we can grow with our customers.

Developer’s APIs

We want to make things as simple as possible for developers, without limiting what can be accomplished. We built our trading screen on the same APIs that you can use to integrate our services in to your own web applications. Our APIS are accessible over HTTPS and are fully REST-ful for security and flexibility.
